What is Gambody?

Gambody is the marketplace where you can buy and sell 3D printing files of characters and objects from renowned and popular video games.

3D marketplace of computer/video game related models for 3D printing

Relying on the growing video/computer gaming trend, Gambody has thought to come to the help of numerous video game fans and 3D printing enthusiasts and came up with the idea of creating a platform for 3D printing models of their favorite heroes, war vehicles, aircrafts, cars and many more, from their beloved video/computer games, to make them emerge from their computer screen into the physical world.

We focus on a narrow niche of video/computer games related characters and items, as it is something that identifies with the hobby we have been enjoying for several years now. Being huge gamers ourselves, we accepted the challenge of creating an online hub where game fans from all around the world could gather and buy, download, and share exclusive and unique 3D printable files of their favorite heroes and outstanding game vehicles and machines.

It is easy to join our ever expanding, highly-customizable platform. You can either sign up with your email address, Facebook or Twitter account. You will then be provided with your personal page, where you can upload your own 3D printing files of amazingly crafted models, or you can download other modelers’ files and print them on your 3D printer. It is up to you what you do next with these stunning character models: place them on your desktop or shelf, surprise others or keep adding them to your impressive collection.

Buy and sell .STL files for 3D printers

Gambody’s in-house and third party contributing designers are constantly expanding the range of 3D print video game models for sale with new ones added weekly. All 3D printing files for models showcased on our marketplace are zipped in .STL format and are adapted for SLS/SLA and FFF printers. We make sure to create an effortless, smooth and error-free printing experience for all our customers. We also provide detailed technical and printing instructions, following a thorough testing procedure of all files created by our in-house designers and downloadable from our platform.

We are happy to welcome all 3D technology enthusiasts on our platform, where you can get a thorough insight into what soon will become a groundswell. Now you can be part of a 3D print marketplace that knows no creative boundaries, either as a buyer or seller.
